# My journey with C++ and Object-Oriented Prgramming.
My journey with C++ began in my second year of study I was first introduced to OOP concepts like
 -classes
 -inheritance
 -polymorphism
 -encapsulation
These concepts were challenging but they gradually reshaped the way I approached problem solving.
C++ for me was not just another programming language; It required discipline, precision, and a deeper understanding of how software works internally.

As I progressed, I began to appreaciate how C++ forces developers to think about memory management, efficiency, and system behavior. Unlike higher-level languages that abstract many details, C++ exposed me to the underlying mechanics of computing. This strenghthened my understanding of performance, architecture, design, and low level system interaction.

Through academic projects and personal experimentation, I have continously developed a strong interest in building structured, efficient and scalable systems. 
My exposure to OOP and C++ has built a solid foundation that influences how I design software today. 
It has also helped in other topics like: 
 -Data structures and algorithms and investigating their efficiency. 
 -Communication and Systems in topics like: Interprocess communication(IPC), Remote invocation and     Indirect communication, etc.
 -Web programming and applications

I can't express enough how Love OOP, it has shaped my skills and my mindset and has taught me to be patient and creative. Next I will now dive now to the TreeFrog Framework for my research in web programming and applications.
